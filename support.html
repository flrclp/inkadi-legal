Contact us
Email hello@inkadi.app — we typically reply within a couple of business days. Please include your device and iOS version so we can help faster.

Common questions

Where is my data stored? On your device only. Inkadi has no account and no server — your clients and appointments never leave your phone.

How do reminders work? Inkadi writes the message and hands it to your phone’s Messages or share sheet (SMS, Messenger, WhatsApp). Your client doesn’t need the app.

How do I manage or cancel Inkadi Pro? Open the Settings (App Store) app → tap your name → Subscriptions → Inkadi. You can cancel there anytime.

I switched phones — where are my appointments? Because data is stored locally, it doesn’t automatically transfer between devices. Keep your own notes of important bookings until device sync is added.
